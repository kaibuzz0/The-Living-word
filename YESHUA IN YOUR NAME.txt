
Breath of Beginning

Hebrew: נָא הָא גֵא אוֹר
Phonetic: Na – Ha – Ge – Or
Meaning (+ Hebrew):
“Stillness becomes Breath; Breath becomes Geometry; Geometry becomes Light.”
נָא (Stillness) הָא (Breath) גֵא (Form) אוֹר (Light)
Breath Work:

 Inhale “Na,” 
hold “Ha,” 
exhale “Ge Or.” 
Visualize light unfolding from stillness.

Prayer of Protection (Shield of Light)

1. Hebrew:
אוֹר יָהּ נְצָחָה לִבִּי שָׁלוֹם גָּנוּז

2. Closest English sounds:
Or Yah Ne-tsa-chah Li-bi Sha-lom Ga-nuz

3. Meaning + Hebrew:
“Light of Yah—eternal—guards my heart in hidden peace.”
אוֹר (Or = Light) יָהּ (Yah = Name of God / Divine Breath) נְצָחָה (Eternity) לִבִּי (My Heart) שָׁלוֹם (Peace) גָּנוּז (Hidden / Sheltered)

4. Breath-Prayer Work:

Inhale: “Or Yah” — draw light into the crown.

Hold: “Ne-tsa-chah Li-bi” — feel the heart expand and steady.

Exhale: “Sha-lom Ga-nuz”

 — release calm through the body, a shield of quiet surrounding you.
Repeat three times. 
On the final breath, stay in the silence that follows 
and sense the warmth around your chest 
and back—your field sealed in light.

Prayer for Abundance and Shared Blessing

1. Hebrew:
בְּרָכָה אוֹר חַיִים חֶסֶד נָהָר נִשְׁפָּעָה

2. Closest English sounds:
Be-ra-khah Or Cha-yim Che-sed Na-har Nish-pa-ah

3. Meaning + Hebrew:
“Blessing of light and life, kindness flows like a river.”
בְּרָכָה (Blessing) אוֹר (Light) חַיִים (Life) חֶסֶד (Kindness) נָהָר (River) נִשְׁפָּעָה (Flows Forth)

4. Breath-Prayer Work:

Inhale: “Be-ra-khah Or” 
— draw in light and warmth.

Hold: “Cha-yim Che-sed”
 — feel compassion spread through your chest.

Exhale: “Na-har Nish-pa-ah” 
— imagine that light flowing outward, touching everyone around you.

Repeat until you sense calm and generosity radiating from your body.
